Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of chicken breast
  • honey mustard dressing
  • bread crumbs
  • parm cheese
  • favorite spaghetti sauce

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Let chicken marinate in honey mustard for 20 minutes
  3. Roll in a 50/50 mix of bread crumbs/parm cheese
  4. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes
  5. Last 2 minutes, top with provolone place atop spaghetti and sauce
