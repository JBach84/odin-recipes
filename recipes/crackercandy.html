Cracker Candy

Ingredients:

  • 1 tube saltines
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 1/8 cup brown sugar
  • 1 bag choclate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Line crackers end to end on cookie sheet
  3. In saucepan, melt butter and brown sugar on low heat
  4. Continue stirring until boiling, then boil for 3 minutes
  5. Pour over crackers evenly, working quickly
  6. Put sheet in the oven for 5 minutes
  7. Remove, pour chocolate chips and let melt for 3 minutes
  8. Spread melted chocolate over the top evenly
