Lemon Chess Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Butter room temperature
  • 2 cups Sugar
  • 3 tablespoon Flour
  • 3 ea Egg
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon Lemon Extract
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon Butter Flavoring
  • 1 each unbaked pie crust (9oz)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cream butter and sugar, then add the flour
  3. Add eggs, one at a time, beating after each egg
  4. Slowly beat in the buttermilk and the flavorings, mixing well
  5. Pour into pie crust and bake for 55 minutes
  6. The middle may be a little soft when you take it out, but it will firm up as it cools
